What we offer

Programs

Competitive Robotics

Home to three competitive teams: FIRST LEGO League for students ages 9–14 who design, build, and program LEGO robots for each season's missions, and FIRST Tech Challenge for older students engineering a full-size metal robot in CAD and real life. Teams meet weekly from September through the competition season.

Classes & Camps

Seasonal camps and one-off workshops covering robotics, 3D printing, CAD, and more. Schedules and registration are announced as sessions open — reach out below to get on the list.

Field Trips & Outreach Events

We bring the Forge to you — school visits, library programs, and community events across Del Norte and Curry Counties, plus field trips to our space for classes and youth groups.

Redwood Rumblers Coming soon

Our antweight combat robotics league. Design a bot, 3D print and build it, then battle it in the arena. Great for builders who like a little (controlled) chaos.

Crescent Crawlers RC Club Coming soon

Radio-controlled crawler trucks on real terrain. Learn driving skill, vehicle tuning, and basic electronics in a laid-back club setting.

Who we are

About Us

Founded in 2024 and now in our third year, Redwoods Wonder Forge is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit with a mission to provide high-quality STEAM experiences, education, and opportunities for youth in Del Norte and Curry Counties. Through classes, camps, clubs, community events, school visits, and three competitive robotics teams, we're the only organization within 100 miles offering competitive robotics to young people in this region.

We serve a rural region where every district school holds Title I status and more than 60% of students qualify for free and reduced lunch — so access is at the heart of everything we do. In our first two years we served more than 150 youth in-house and reached over 1,000 through community events, and two of our three robotics teams advanced to state championships. Partnerships with community organizations, local charter schools, and business sponsors let us offer scholarships and free events across the region.

We are a completely 100% volunteer-run organization with zero paid staff. Every coach, mentor, and board member donates their time, so every dollar you give goes directly to programs, tools, and materials for young makers. If you'd like to volunteer, sponsor, or donate, we'd love to hear from you.
